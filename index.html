Hi!

Carmel School, Peyad was founded and managed by the Carmelite Sisters of St. Teresa , CSST Congregation in the year 2005. It has now spread its roots and established itself as a mighty Institution of learning. Our school is located at Peyad. We create an environment which will contribute to the realisation of the full potential of students, aided by the love and support of parents and the society. The school is well known for its achievements in the field of academics, athletics and extracurricular activities. In the field of arts and sports the school has achieved an enviable position. The hard work and sacrifice of many are relfected in the results achieved by the school in various spheres. Our school is environmental friendly school. ‘Green world Dream world ‘ is our dream Every aspect of green protocol is looked into with great care and attention, right from banning the use of plastic, re-cycling waste water, rain water harvesting and promoting planting of saplings in school campus as well as in the locality and public places. Our school has been successful in gaining the British council international school Award and its accredited for the period of 2018-2021
